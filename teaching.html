Teaching

This year I will be teaching a short course on ecological modelling for UCL's research masters course in Biodiversity, Evolution and Conservation, and on a 3rd/4th year undergraduate course in conservation biology. More information on these will follow.

I have delivered guest lectures for Oxford's masters course in Biodiversity, Conservation and Management, and for an undergraduate course in Conservation at Nottingham University.

I ran a training course for Imperial College London masters students, on the basics of the use of mixed-effects models for modelling biodiversity responses to land-use change.
