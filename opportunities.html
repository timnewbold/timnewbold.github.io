PhD Enquiries:

I am always interested to here from potential PhD students and post-docs. If you are interested, please contact me and send a CV.

Current Opportunities:

We are looking for a post-doc interested in understanding how global environmental changes are impacting the structure of ecosystems, to come and work with us on the Madingley General Ecosystem Model. The project is a collaboration with James Rosindell at Imperial College London. We are combining the strengths of ecological neutral theory and the Madingley Model to make novel predictions about the effects of land-use and climate change on the diversity of terrestrial ecosystems worldwide. You will join a supportive research group of around 15 members tacking similar questions, within the larger Centre for Biodiversity and Environment Research at UCL. Ideally, we are looking for someone with experience of theoretical ecological modelling and computer coding. For more information please send an email, or click here to apply.
